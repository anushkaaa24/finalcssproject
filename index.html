Darshan Raval

Darshan Raval from ahmedabad born on 18 oct 1994, is an Indian Singer composer, and songwriter. He is known for his work in Hindi, Gujarati,Punjabi and Bengali. He was runner-up in the 2014 StarPlus reality music show India's Raw Star.

The versatile singer continued releasing several singles including Tera Zikr, Asal Mein, Ek Tarfa,Judaiyaan and Tu Hai.Despite his tender age in his musical career at 28-years-old, he has sung more than 100 songs in different languages..
